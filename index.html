Travel to Tottori Prefecture

Where is it?

Tottori Prefecture (鳥取県 Tottori-ken) is a prefecture of Japan located in the Chūgoku region. The capital is the city of Tottori. It is the least populous prefecture in Japan.

Noted Places

  • Daisen

    • Daisen, the highest of the Chūgoku Mountains, 1,729 m (5,673 ft).

    • A street in Sakaiminato dedicated to Shigeru Mizuki's GeGeGe no Kitaro manga character.
